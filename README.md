### üìä Telegram Channel Ad Effectiveness Analytics

TgAnalytics is a comprehensive platform for analyzing Telegram channels' advertising effectiveness, pricing, and ROI metrics. Unlike similar services such as telega.in, our platform specifically focuses on providing transparent data about advertising costs and effectiveness, helping advertisers make informed decisions about channel selection.

### üéØ Target Audience

- **Advertisers and marketers**: find the most cost-effective channels based on actual performance data
- **Business owners**: optimize advertising budget allocation with transparent ROI metrics
- **Media buyers**: compare channel prices and performance metrics in one place
- **Telegram channel owners**: benchmark pricing and performance against competitors
- **Bloggers and influencers**: evaluate potential collaboration platforms

### ‚ö°Ô∏è Features

- **Transparent ad pricing**: Actual prices from Telegram channels
- **ROI analytics**: See which channels provide the best return on ad investment
- **Performance metrics**: ERR, CPM, cost per subscriber, and engagement rates
- **Price comparison**: Compare advertising costs across similar channels
- **Channel database**: Comprehensive information about thousands of Telegram channels
- **Ad effectiveness score**: Proprietary algorithm to evaluate channel ad performance
- **Market price trends**: Track how prices change over time
- **Audience quality assessment**: Evaluate real reach vs. subscriber numbers
- **Performance history**: See how channels performed over time
- **Custom analytics**: Generate reports based on your specific needs

### üîÑ Ad Effectiveness Score Explained

Our Ad Effectiveness Score provides a comprehensive evaluation of a channel's advertising performance:

- Combines multiple factors: actual reach, engagement rate, audience quality, and price
- Lower score means better cost-efficiency (more value for your ad spend)
- Values are benchmarked against current market data:
  - **< 0.7**: Excellent (green) - significantly better ROI than market average
  - **0.7 - 0.9**: Good (light green) - better ROI than market average
  - **0.9 - 1.1**: Normal (yellow) - average market ROI
  - **1.1 - 1.5**: Below average (orange) - worse ROI than market average
  - **> 1.5**: Poor (red) - significantly worse ROI than market average

### üìà Market Data 

The platform provides up-to-date market data for Telegram advertising:

**Average prices by channel size:**
- Micro (up to 10K): $0.5-2 CPM
- Small (10K-50K): $2-4 CPM
- Medium (50K-200K): $4-7 CPM
- Large (200K-1M): $7-12 CPM
- Extra large (1M+): $12-20+ CPM

**Effectiveness metrics tracked:**
- Views-to-subscribers ratio
- Engagement rate (reactions, comments, forwards)
- Click-through rate on advertising posts
- Subscriber acquisition cost
- Ad post retention time (how long the ad stays visible)
- Real audience reach percentage

- **UI components**: shadcn/ui
- **Data storage**: Database + localStorage for user preferences
- **Analytics**: Custom metrics calculation engine
