### üìä Telegram Channel Analytics Aggregator

TgAnalytics is a comprehensive data aggregation platform that collects and analyzes advertising metrics across thousands of Telegram channels. By aggregating pricing data, engagement rates, and ROI metrics from multiple sources, we provide advertisers with the most complete market overview available, helping them make data-driven decisions when selecting channels for promotion.

### üéØ Target Audience

- **Advertisers and marketers**: access aggregated data from thousands of channels to find the most cost-effective advertising options
- **Business owners**: compare ROI across the entire Telegram advertising market
- **Media buyers**: quickly access pricing trends and benchmark data across different niches
- **Telegram channel owners**: understand competitive positioning through aggregated market data
- **Bloggers and influencers**: evaluate potential collaboration platforms based on comprehensive market data

### ‚ö°Ô∏è Features

- **Market-wide data aggregation**: Collect and normalize advertising data across the Telegram ecosystem
- **Comprehensive price benchmarking**: Compare your channels against aggregated market rates
- **Performance aggregation**: See how channels perform relative to industry and niche averages
- **Price trend analysis**: Track how advertising rates change over time across the entire market
- **Channel discovery engine**: Find channels with the best ROI based on aggregated performance data
- **Market health indicators**: Monitor the overall state of the Telegram advertising market
- **Aggregated audience insights**: Understand audience behavior patterns across channels
- **Competitive analysis**: Benchmark channels against aggregated performance metrics
- **Industry-specific data**: View aggregated metrics filtered by niche or industry
- **API access**: Connect directly to our aggregated data for your own analytics

### üîÑ Aggregated Effectiveness Score Explained

Our Aggregated Effectiveness Score combines data from thousands of channels to create benchmarks for advertising performance:

- Based on millions of aggregated data points across the Telegram ecosystem
- Normalized against industry-specific benchmarks derived from our aggregated database
- Provides a comparative score showing how a channel performs against aggregated market data:
  - **< 0.7**: Excellent (green) - significantly outperforms aggregated market data
  - **0.7 - 0.9**: Good (light green) - better than aggregated market averages
  - **0.9 - 1.1**: Normal (yellow) - aligns with aggregated market data
  - **1.1 - 1.5**: Below average (orange) - worse than aggregated market data
  - **> 1.5**: Poor (red) - significantly underperforms against aggregated metrics

### üìà Aggregated Market Data 

Our platform aggregates and provides up-to-date market data across the entire Telegram advertising ecosystem:

**Aggregated prices by channel size:**
- Micro (up to 10K): $0.5-2 CPM (aggregated from 5,000+ channels)
- Small (10K-50K): $2-4 CPM (aggregated from 8,000+ channels)
- Medium (50K-200K): $4-7 CPM (aggregated from 3,500+ channels)
- Large (200K-1M): $7-12 CPM (aggregated from 1,200+ channels)
- Extra large (1M+): $12-20+ CPM (aggregated from 500+ channels)

**Aggregated performance metrics:**
- Views-to-subscribers ratio (collected from 18,000+ channels)
- Engagement rates (aggregated from 15 million+ posts)
- Click-through rates (normalized from 3 million+ advertising campaigns)
- Audience quality scores (derived from aggregated engagement data)
- Message retention metrics (aggregated from 12,000+ advertising posts)
- Real reach percentages (collected from 9,000+ verified channels)
